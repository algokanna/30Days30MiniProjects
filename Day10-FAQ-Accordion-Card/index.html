FAQ

  • How many team members can I invite?
    You can invite 5 team members with the Professional Plan.
  • What is the maximum file upload size?
    No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your alloted storage space.
  • How do I reset my password?
    You can reset your password by clicking on reset password in Profile Settings or clicking on Forget My Password in the Login Window. We'll send you an email to guide you through to process to reset your password.
  • Can I cancel my subscription?
    Yes, you can cancel your subscription at anytime you want.
  • Do you provide additional support?
    24/7 support is only available to Professional Plan and Enterprise Plan
